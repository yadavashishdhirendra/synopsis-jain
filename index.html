Title: Full-Stack Food Ordering System with Admin Management - Leveraging MERN Stack Technology

Synopsis:

The Full-Stack Food Ordering System with Admin Management is a comprehensive web application that aims to transform the online food ordering experience. This project utilizes the MERN stack technology (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) to develop a robust and scalable platform that allows customers to order food from their favorite restaurants and provides restaurant owners with an intuitive administration panel to manage their menus, orders, and overall business operations.
